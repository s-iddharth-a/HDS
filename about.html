About Home Design Solutions

HDS started as a remodeling effort by Emily and her husband Joe back in May 2007. They had contracted to do work for some family and friends that wanted to upgrade their existing homes (bathrooms and kitchens are their specialty). This included providing design work, and upgrades to kitchens and baths. Emily and Joe quickly determined that there was a tremendous need for this service in the greater Phoenix/Scottsdale, AZ area.

They applied for a business license and opened Home Design Solutions as an LLC out of a small store location they rented at 101 Sedalia Drive, Phoenix, AZ 85001. The first store location included some sample cabinets and appliances that they would later become regional distributors for. Both Joe and Emily became General Contractors in 2007 and all their bath and kitchen design specialists that have been hired since have acquired kitchen and bath design certifications.

Early Growth

They saw rapid growth for their company and hired 2 sales associates to help with the increased demand and 3 cabinet, appliance and bathroom installers and offer delivery services if needed. They added 2 more employees, one to keep track of appointments and one to schedule the work of the installers and renovators. The other employee helped with deliveries and warehousing (they have a very limited warehouse and cannot stock items for more than 2-3 weeks).

They became so busy with their construction, design and consultation services and trying to run a successful small business that they hired a design/sales professional to take over much of their design work.

Expansion

In 2009, they opened a second location at 901 S. Sedona Way, Scottsdale, AZ. This was a much smaller location and included only 4 employees (2 sales and design associates and 2 installation/renovation and delivery associates). Since that time the Scottsdale location has added 2 more installation/renovation and delivery associates, 1 office manager, and 1 store manager that could also do sales and design work.

In 2012, they opened their third location in Tucson, AZ, at 8023 Sedalia Dr., Phoenix, AZ. They followed the same pattern as the Scottsdale location but hired all the employees at one time (7 employees total, similar to the Scottsdale location).

National Expansion

Their latest endeavor, in June 2016, was the purchase of 2 existing stores out of state:

  • Elite Home Renovations in Las Vegas, NV – a high-end appliance and bathroom specialist.
  • IRB Home Supply in Albuquerque, NM – a typical home supply store.

Elite has a more sophisticated system, with a server running Windows Server 2016, and 4 workstations connected through an 8-port Linksys Switch running Windows 10.

Representing Manufacturers

HDS represents a wide range of manufacturers that offer cutting-edge products. You can browse current models and technical specifications on their website. They aim to give customers direct access to the products that interest them most.

Financial Success

HDS has proven to be a financial success, with total gross revenue exceeding $14.5 million. They operate at a gross margin of 30% and spend about $1.775 million annually on salaries and benefits for their 39 employees (including Joe and Emily). Overhead costs amount to about $75,000 per month for all 5 locations, which covers maintenance and upkeep of company vehicles.

They own 10 delivery trucks, with 2 located at each location. If one store requires a delivery, it can use vehicles and staff from other locations as available.

Their dedication to quality, customer service, and expansion has helped make Home Design Solutions one of the leading remodeling services in the region.
